Key Dates

  • 1908: Construction of the Titanic was announced along with its sister ship, Olympic.
  • 31st March 1909: Construction began on the Titanic.
  • 31st May 1911: Engines and interior not yet installed, however the Titanic was launched.
  • 31st March 1912: Construction was completed on the Titanic.
  • 10th April 1912: The Titanic set sail for its maiden voyage.
